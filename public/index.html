Reserved ambitions

Words of theory and practice by Tim Leisio.

Hello, and thank you for visiting my site.

I am currently a User Experience (UX) Lead at Thomson Reuters, specializing in information architecture, currently employed at Thomson Reuters.

In my day-to-day, you'll find me:

  • Leading and facilitating research efforts to understand people
  • Leading and facilitating co-collaborative activities that create alignment between people
  • Heads down; mulling over chaotic information in order create compelling narratives that achieve the goals of both businesses and constituents
  • Promoting consistency in shared language, understanding, and action

While I am extremely thankful and happy that my time, for the last 9 years, has been taken by designing software products, my interests elsewhere are beyond a critical tipping point.

If the skills above are in need, those at the intersection of regenerative development or agriculture, social equality, climate justice, and local government are encouraged to send inquiries to timothyleisio@gmail.com, or reach out to me on linkedin or twitter. I'm eager to take action.
